jazz soul

typeface

The typeface is characterized by its extreme contrast in stroke width and consists exclusively of uppercase letters. This high-contrast typography gives texts a striking, dynamic look and is particularly suitable for headlines and bold designs that aim to capture attention and leave a lasting impression.

bubble tech

typeface

The Bubble Tech typeface is characterized by its soft, rounded shapes and a modern, friendly aesthetic. It combines playful curves with a sturdy, highly legible structure. This futuristic typography is particularly suitable for headlines, branding, and bold designs that aim to create a warm, inviting, yet striking impression.
