limon

laptop friendly café

The idea behind the café is to provide a perfect place for people looking for a productive work environment. With the right atmosphere and surroundings, we create a space where efficient work is possible. Key aspects include long opening hours and optimal equipment. The café is "laptop-friendly" and offers everything needed for efficient work. We specifically target students, pupils, and workers who are looking for a pleasant and supportive place for their activities.

rufer

graphic novel publisher

The graphic novels depict problems, situations, or events that arise in our everyday lives. The stories may be presented in a dramatic or exaggerated manner but with a design that is as simple as possible, using minimal elements. Everyday situations can reflect either cross-cultural or culture-specific issues. The goal is to raise awareness of both familiar and unfamiliar events in society. Even when we are aware of them, we often tend to overlook them.
